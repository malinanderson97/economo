What drives what

Each arrow points from a variable to the one it feeds into, read straight off the model’s solve() and step() functions. Output Y sits at the centre; the warm dashed arrows are the policy feedback loop that pulls the economy back to potential.

Edges are the actual variable dependencies in the code. Closed: Y = isOutput(G,T,r), r = i − πᵉ, π = πᵉ + α(Y−Yₙ)/Yₙ + z, Taylor i ← ρ·i + (1−ρ)[rₙ+π̄ + φ(π−π̄) + ψ·gap], and Yₙ from structural m, z. Open adds E = Eᵉ(1+i)/(1+i*), ε = E·P/P*, and ε, m₁, Y* entering isOutput.
